BBC

The withdrawal is attributed to the regional threat from the ongoing US-Israel war with Iran, with NATO forces reportedly relocating from the Middle East to Europe.

Why it matters: The pullback signals growing European concern about the Iran conflict's regional spillover and marks a shift in coalition priorities away from counter-ISIS operations.

Nigeria Suicide Bombings Kill 23 in Maiduguri

Reuters

At least 23 people were killed and 108 others injured in suspected suicide bombings at three locations in Maiduguri, Borno State, including the Monday Market and University of Maiduguri Teaching Hospital. No group has claimed responsibility for the attacks.

Why it matters: The coordinated attacks underscore Nigeria's persistent security challenges in the northeast, where insurgent groups remain active despite years of military operations.
