OpenAI Closes Historic $110B Funding Round at $730B Valuation

AI Funding Tracker

OpenAI closed the largest private funding round in history with $110 billion at a $730 billion pre-money valuation, led by Amazon ($50B), Nvidia ($30B), and SoftBank ($30B). The deal includes strategic infrastructure commitments with Amazon launching stateful runtime on AWS Bedrock with 2GW of Trainium compute, while Nvidia provides 3GW of inference capacity and 2GW of Vera Rubin training systems. OpenAI now serves over 900 million weekly active users with its post-money valuation reaching approximately $840 billion.

Why It Matters:
This fundamentally restructures the AI compute supply chain by turning major investors into infrastructure partners with preferred access, changing how frontier AI models are deployed and scaled.
